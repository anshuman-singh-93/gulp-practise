In the case of a temporary employment, the Employer shall pay a Success Fee equal to 20% (for greater than 6 months term initial commitment) and 25% (for less than or equal to 6 months term initial commitment) of the Candidate’s monthly salary.   • 12.5% of the gross annual salary of the Candidate if converted after 3 months of temporary placement and before 6 months of temporary placement from its Start Date.
  • 10% of the gross annual salary of the Candidate if converted after 6 months of temporary placement and before 12 months of temporary placement from its Start Date.
  • 5% of the gross annual salary of the Candidate if converted after 12 months of temporary placement and before 24 months of temporary placement from its Start Date.
  • No fee if converted after 24 months of temporary placement from its Start Date.
  • Taxes will be applicable in addition to the above Success Fee.
